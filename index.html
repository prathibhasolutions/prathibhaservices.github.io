OUR SERVICES

PLUMBING

Plumbing work involves installing, repairing, and maintaining water and sewage systems in buildings. This includes pipes, fixtures, and fittings to ensure proper water supply and waste removal.

LOGISTICS

House shifting is the process of moving a residence from one location to another, involving packing, loading, transportation, and unpacking of household belongings for a successful relocation.

CARPENTRY

Carpentry involves shaping, assembling, and installing wooden structures, such as furniture, cabinets, and building frameworks. It requires skills in cutting, joining, and finishing wood materials.

PAINTING

Painting works entail surface preparation, applying primers and coats of paint, and achieving a desired finish on walls, ceilings, or structures to enhance aesthetics and protect surfaces.

TILES WORK

Tile works involve the installation of ceramic, porcelain, or other types of tiles on surfaces such as floors and walls, providing decorative and functional enhancements to spaces.

ELECTRICAL

Electricians install, maintain, and repair electrical systems, including wiring, outlets, and fixtures, to ensure safe and reliable electricity supply in buildings and facilities.

GEYSER REPAIR

Geyser repair involves diagnosing and fixing issues in water heaters, such as leaks, heating coils problem, and thermostat malfunctions, to ensure hot water supply in households.

WASHING MACHINE REPAIR

Washing machine repair includes diagnosing and fixing issues such as motor problems, leaks, electrical faults, to ensure proper functioning and convenience for households.

WATER PURIFIER REPAIR

Water purifier repair involves diagnosing and fixing issues in water filtration systems, like filter replacement, installations or electrical troubleshooting, to ensure clean and safe drinking water.

REFRIGERATOR REPAIR

Refrigerator repair involves diagnosing and fixing issues in cooling systems, such as compressor or thermostat malfunctions, to ensure proper temperature control and preservation of food in households.
